Happiness
Project

Most of us live busy lives. Often, we are busy juggling tasks and responsibilities that we forget to take the time to do the things that bring us happiness. Unfortunately, a lack of joy in your daily life can lead to unhappiness, and it can even have long term negative consequences on your health.

In the process of finding happiness, Yoon Hee Cho documented things that make her happy in her daily life. This website is a response to her experience.
